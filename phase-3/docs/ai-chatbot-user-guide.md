# AI-Powered Todo Chatbot - User Documentation

## Overview

The AI-Powered Todo Chatbot is an intelligent assistant that helps you manage your tasks using natural language. Simply talk to the bot as you would with a friend, and it will create, update, and manage your tasks accordingly.

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to the dashboard
2. Find the chat interface
3. Start typing your requests in natural language
4. The AI will process your request and respond accordingly

## Supported Commands

### Creating Tasks
- "Add a task to buy groceries"
- "Create a task to finish the report by Friday"
- "New task: schedule dentist appointment"
- "I need to call mom tomorrow"
- "Remind me to water plants next week"

### Updating Tasks
- "Change the grocery task to include organic items"
- "Update the meeting task to include Zoom link"
- "Modify the workout task to be in the morning"

### Completing Tasks
- "Mark the laundry task as complete"
- "Complete the dishes task"
- "Finish the vacuuming task"
- "Check off the shopping task"

### Deleting Tasks
- "Delete the old meeting task"
- "Remove the cancelled appointment"
- "Get rid of the obsolete reminder"

### Viewing Tasks
- "Show me my tasks"
- "List all my tasks"
- "What are my current tasks?"
- "Display my pending tasks"

### Getting Suggestions
- "What should I work on today?"
- "Any suggestions for tasks?"
- "Recommend something productive to do"

## Natural Language Tips

The AI is designed to understand various ways of expressing your needs:

- **Be Specific**: "Add a high priority task to prepare presentation for Monday's meeting" 
- **Include Dates**: "Create a task to call insurance company by end of week"
- **Mention Priority**: "I have an urgent task to follow up with client"
- **Contextual References**: After creating a task, you can refer to it as "it" or "that task"

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**AI doesn't understand my request**
- Try rephrasing using simpler language
- Be more specific about what you want
- Example: Instead of "Handle that thing," say "Add a task to schedule the meeting"

**Task not created as expected**
- Check that your request includes clear action words like "add," "create," or "new"
- Make sure the task title is clear and specific

**Wrong date or priority assigned**
- Be explicit about dates and priority levels
- Example: "Add a high priority task to finish report by tomorrow"

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues:
1. Try rephrasing your request
2. Use the example commands above as templates
3. Contact support if problems persist

## Best Practices

- Use clear, action-oriented language
- Include specific dates and times when relevant
- Take advantage of AI suggestions to boost productivity
- Review AI-created tasks to ensure accuracy
- Regularly check your privacy settings

## Limitations

- The AI understands English primarily
- Very complex or ambiguous requests may not be processed correctly
- The AI cannot access external calendars or systems beyond this application
- Suggestions are based on patterns and may not always align with your current needs
